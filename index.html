Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding and hiring an artist in Nigeria.

How do I commission an artist in Nigeria?

Browse artist profiles on our directory, review their portfolios and pricing, then contact them directly via WhatsApp or their booking form. Share your reference photo, discuss your vision, agree on pricing and timeline, and the artist will begin creating your piece.

How much does a custom portrait cost in Nigeria?

Portrait pricing varies depending on the medium (oil, acrylic, pencil, charcoal), size, number of subjects, and the artist's experience level. Expect anywhere from ₦30,000 for a simple pencil sketch to ₦500,000+ for large oil paintings. Contact artists directly for accurate quotes.

Do Nigerian artists ship internationally?

Yes. Many artists on our directory offer worldwide shipping to the UK, USA, Canada, France, and other countries. Artworks are securely packaged with protective framing. Ask your chosen artist about international delivery options and costs.

How long does it take to complete a portrait?

Timelines depend on the medium and complexity. Pencil portraits typically take 5 to 10 days. Oil paintings may take 7 to 21 days. Rush options are available with some artists. Always confirm the timeline before commissioning.

How can I list my art business on this directory?

We are currently accepting applications from professional artists across Nigeria. Scroll down to the waitlist form and submit your details. We review every application to ensure quality for our clients.

Is Artist in Nigeria free to use?

Browsing and contacting artists is completely free for clients. Artists pay a listing fee to maintain their profile on the directory. This keeps our platform sustainable and ensures only serious, professional artists are listed.

What areas of Nigeria do you cover?

We cover all 36 Nigerian states and the Federal Capital Territory. Whether you need an artist in Lagos, a muralist in Abuja, or a portrait painter in Bayelsa, our directory connects you with verified professionals across the country.
